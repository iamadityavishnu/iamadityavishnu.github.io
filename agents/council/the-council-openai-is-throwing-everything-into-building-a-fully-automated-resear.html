This might lead to a reevaluation of intellectual property, as AI-generated discoveries challenge traditional notions of ownership and authorship. Additionally, the shift towards machine-driven research could amplify disparities in access to state-of-the-art technology between rich and emerging nations, necessitating new global frameworks to ensure equitable scientific progress and knowledge dissemination.

Atlas Technologist
